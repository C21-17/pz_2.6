Lethal Company

You are a contracted worker for the Company. Your job is to collect scrap from abandoned, industrialized moons to meet the Company's profit quota. You can use the cash you earn to travel to new moons with higher risks and rewards--or you can buy fancy suits and decorations for your ship. Experience nature, scanning any creature you find to add them to your bestiary. Explore the wondrous outdoors and rummage through their derelict, steel and concrete underbellies. Just never miss the quota. These dangers prey upon the vulnerable and lonesome, and the protection of your crew may be your only hope. You can guide your crewmates from your ship, using the radar to call out traps and using the ship's terminal to access remotely locked doors--or you can all go in together. The Company store has many useful tools for the job, including lights, shovels, walkie talkies, stun grenades, or boomboxes. Things get dangerous at night. Communicate with your crewmates to carry all valuables to the ship before things get too dangerous, and try not to leave anyone behind.

Counter-Strike 2

For more than two decades, Counter-Strike has exemplified the world's premier competitive gaming experience, driven by millions of players around the world. Now it's time for a new phase - Counter-Strike 2. Counter-Strike 2 is a free upgrade for CS:GO that marks the biggest technological leap in the history of the series. Powered by the Source 2 engine, it modernizes the game with realistic and physically based rendering, state-of-the-art networking, and improved community workshop tools.

Call of Duty

is a series of video games in the first-person shooter genre , dedicated to the Second World War , the Cold War , the fight against terrorism , the hypothetical Third World War, future and space wars. The series consists of seventeen main games and twelve spin-offs. Initially, the series was created for PC , later it began to be published on game consoles and portable devices . Call of Duty games are published by Activision and Aspyr Media (the latter is publishing for the Apple macOS platform ). The main games of the series are created by Infinity Ward , Treyarch , Sledgehammer Games , Raven Software . Gray Matter Interactive , Spark Unlimited , Pi Studios , and Amaze Entertainment participated in the creation of other versions and editions of the game at different times, using a large number of different technologies. In addition to the games themselves, other products are produced under the Call of Duty franchise. Plan -B Toys produces figurines from the game, Upper Deck produces card sets for the game, and WildStorm publishes the comics.;
